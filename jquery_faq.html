National Park FAQ
How many people visit the National Parks each year?
In 2021 297,115,406 people visited the National Parks.
What is the most visited National Park?
Blue Ridge Parkway was the most visited National Park in 2021 with 15.9 million visits.
Who is the director of the National Park Service?
Charles F. Sams III is the director of the National Park Service.
How many employees are in the National Park Service?
Around 20,000 employees and more than 279,000 volunteers.
Which National Park was the nations first?
Yellowstone National Park is the nations first ever National Park.
How many acres are in the National Park System?
Over 85 million acres of land are in the National Park System.
What is the largest National Park?
Wrangell-St. Elias National Park and Reserve in Alaska is the largest National Park.
What is the smallest National Park?
Thaddeus Kosciuszko National Memorial is the smallest National Park.
How many of the highest waterfalls are located at Yosemite National Park?
3 of the highest waterfalls in the world are located at Yosemite National Park.
Which of the National Parks contains one of the Natural Wonders of the World?
The Grand Canyon in Grand Canyon National Park is one of the 7 Natural Wonders of the World.
